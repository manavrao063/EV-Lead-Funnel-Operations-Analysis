**EV Lead Funnel & Operations Analysis**

This project analyzes lead-to-delivery operational data from EV mobility centers to identify bottlenecks in the sales funnel, delivery process, and customer experience.

The goal of the analysis is to uncover conversion gaps, operational inefficiencies, and cancellation drivers, and recommend improvements that can increase revenue, reduce turnaround time (TAT), and enhance customer satisfaction.

-----------------------------------
**Problem Statement**

Electric vehicle dealerships rely heavily on efficient lead management and smooth delivery operations to convert leads into successful deliveries.

However, inefficiencies in lead qualification, delivery timelines, and customer communication can lead to higher cancellations, lower conversions, and poor customer experience.

This project aims to analyze the lead-to-delivery funnel and operational performance across mobility centers to identify key operational gaps and improvement opportunities.

------------------------

EV mobility centers manage a multi-stage funnel from lead generation to vehicle delivery. However, delays in delivery and poor lead handling can reduce conversion rates and increase cancellations.

Analyze the operational data to:

- Evaluate conversion rates across the funnel

- Identify key causes of cancellations

- Understand delivery turnaround time (TAT)

- Assess customer satisfaction trends

Cleaned and analyzed lead-to-delivery dataset using Excel.

Used pivot tables and KPI dashboards to evaluate:

  - Conversion rates

  - Cancellation patterns

  - Delivery turnaround time (TAT)

  - Customer satisfaction (NPS)

Identified key bottlenecks such as:

  -High cancellations due to "Changed Mind"

  - Cold lead mismanagement

  - Delays in the delivery stage

Developed data-driven operational recommendations to address these issues.

**Result**

The analysis identified actionable opportunities to:

- Improve lead qualification and engagement

- Reduce cancellations

- Optimize delivery turnaround time

- Improve customer satisfaction across mobility centers

Projected impact from the recommendations includes:

- ~10–15% potential revenue growth

- 30–40% reduction in cancellations

- Improvement in delivery turnaround time

-------------------------------------
**Key Insights**

Lead-to-delivery conversion rate was ~72.9%

Delivery stage had the longest turnaround time

A significant portion of cancellations were due to customer expectation gaps

Cold leads resulted in very low conversion efficiency

-------------------------------------
**Proposed Solutions**

Implement lead scoring and qualification systems

Improve customer engagement before booking confirmation

Optimize delivery logistics and coordination

Standardize best practices across mobility centers
